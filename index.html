My Final year project
(Happy Pet)

For my final year project I was able to develop a virtual pet game with the target of the game being young children.
The goal of the game is to teach children to understand pet care through the guidance of a vet character who will provide help to progress through the game and educational information on the topics surrounding pet care.
The game includes customisation features such as choosing a name for the pet and Three playable levels which the player can interact with the goal of the game being is to keep a certain number of needs meters up to keep the pet healthy and happy.
The levels contain various mini games based on important aspects of pet care such as affection, diet and exercise in order to convey how the acts of pet care can be carried out and how they function in a way that is entertaining and engaging to the players.
